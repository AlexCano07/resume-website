About Me

Welcome to my digital command center. I'm Alex Cano — a bilingual data analyst, cyber specialist, and systems engineer with a passion for building intelligent tools and repairing what others replace.

I earned my B.S. in Cybersecurity from Southern New Hampshire University, and I’m currently pursuing an M.S. in Business Analytics & Artificial Intelligence at Johns Hopkins University and an M.S. in Electrical Engineering at the University of Virginia.

I build dashboards for the U.S. Department of State, prototype hardware in Blender, mod consoles, and develop local AI systems like Jarvis+ with speech and memory. If it plugs in or runs code, I'm probably tweaking it.
